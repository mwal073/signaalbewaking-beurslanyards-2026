10. Quality Requirements 质量要求

  • Cards must have casino-grade feel — smooth, flexible, durable
  • Colors must match Pantone 200 C for red — provide physical swatch
  • Card back pattern must be perfectly aligned — no visible offset between front and back
  • Rounded corners: 3mm radius
  • Bleed: 3mm on all sides
  • Box must fit cards snugly — not too tight, not too loose
  • Sample/proof required before full production run
